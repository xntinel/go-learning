# Go Concepts

Hands-on exercises covering Go's core concepts through progressive, practical challenges. Each exercise works through a realistic scenario in complete, runnable programs, then closes with the mistakes that scenario invites and an unguided challenge to build on your own.

## Modules

| Module | Sections | Exercises | Description |
|--------|----------|-----------|-------------|
| [Concurrency](concurrency/concurrency.md) | 9 | 123 | Goroutines, channels, select, sync primitives, atomics, context, patterns, errgroup, and race detection |
